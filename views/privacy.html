Privacy Policy


#ShareMyMixtape is an open-source project that allows users to share their top 5 Spotify tracks with others. When using the app, you grant permission for access to your Spotify account username and data solely for the purpose of displaying your top 5 tracks. It's important to note that no data is collected or stored, the API simply displays the authorized information from your Spotify account.

To protect your privacy, appropriate measures are taken to secure your data. If you have any questions or concerns about the privacy policy, please feel free to contact the developer. The focus is to ensure a positive user experience while keeping data secure.
