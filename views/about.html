About


To use, log in to your Spotify account and the app will sort your top tracks. No additional data is collected or stored.

Once your mixtape is generated, you can easily share it with others by saving the image. To save your mixtape, click the download button located at the top of the mixtape.

If you have any questions or concerns about the app, please feel free to contact the developer.
